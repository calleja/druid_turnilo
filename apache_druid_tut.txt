  "documentation": "https://druid.apache.org/docs/latest/ingestion/ingestion-spec/",

Submit the spec to the coordinator-overlord (port 8081), not the broker. In docker-compose.droplet.yml, the coordinator runs coordinator-overlord, which includes the Overlord role — the Overlord is the service that accepts and schedules ingestion tasks. The broker (8082) handles queries only.
coordinator-overlord endpoint: POST http://localhost:8081/druid/indexer/v1/task

To confirm job:
# List running tasks
curl http://localhost:8081/druid/indexer/v1/runningTasks

# List completed tasks
curl http://localhost:8081/druid/indexer/v1/completedTasks
